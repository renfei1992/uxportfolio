Ansys Career Site Redesign

My Role

Ansys is one of the most innovative companies that helps thousands of product designers design better products. The greatest value of Ansys is its talent. However, the design of current career site does not reflect the innovative side of its product personality, so I led the effort to conduct heuristics evaluation and redesign the career site focusing on clean and streamlined UX.

User Experience Redesign

While iterating on the product icons, our HR department reached out to the UX team seeking improvements on the career site. I was assigned to rethink the workflow and redesign the pages.
